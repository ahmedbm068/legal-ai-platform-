Agreement ID: MSA-AR-NL-2026-004  
Effective Date: January 12, 2026  
Parties:
- Atlas Retail Group SARL ("Atlas"), represented by CEO Karim Ben Salem
- Nova Logistics Tunisia SARL ("Nova"), represented by Director Lina Mzoughi

## 1. Scope of Services
Nova will provide inbound warehousing, order packing, and national last-mile delivery services for Atlas ecommerce orders.

## 2. Service Levels (SLA)
- On-time delivery target: 96% monthly.
- Lost package maximum threshold: 0.8% monthly.
- Escalation response time: 4 business hours.
- Breach trigger: if SLA target is missed for two consecutive months, Atlas may issue a formal notice of non-compliance.

## 3. Commercial Terms
- Monthly fixed operations fee: 21,500 TND.
- Variable delivery fee: 8.70 TND per delivered order.
- Fuel surcharge cap: 4.5%.
- Payment terms: Net 30 from invoice date.
- Late payment interest: 1.5% per month after due date.

## 4. Invoicing and Audit
- Invoice issue date: no later than the 5th day of each month.
- Atlas may audit supporting delivery logs with 5 business days prior notice.
- Disputed invoice lines must be notified within 10 calendar days of receipt.

## 5. Data Protection and Confidentiality
- Customer personal data may only be processed for order fulfillment.
- Data retention for delivery proof records: 24 months.
- Security incident reporting deadline: within 24 hours of discovery.

## 6. Term and Termination
- Initial term: 12 months from Effective Date.
- Auto-renewal: 12 months unless either party gives 30 days written notice before renewal date.
- Immediate termination permitted in case of material breach not cured within 15 days after written notice.

## 7. Liability
- Service credits apply if on-time delivery drops below 94%.
- Liability cap: direct damages capped at three months of average fees.
- Liability cap does not apply to fraud, gross negligence, or intentional misconduct.

## 8. Dispute Resolution
- Governing law: Tunisia.
- Step 1: executive negotiation within 10 business days.
- Step 2: mediation in Tunis.
- Step 3: if unresolved, arbitration under agreed commercial arbitration rules.

## 9. Operational Timeline
- January 12, 2026: Agreement signed.
- February 1, 2026: Go-live date.
- February 5, 2026: First invoice due to be issued.
- March 7, 2026: Atlas sent first SLA warning email.
- March 18, 2026: Nova acknowledged temporary routing disruption.

## 10. Signatures
Signed in Tunis on January 12, 2026 by authorized representatives of both parties.
